Looking for Something?

Where can I get the app?

Kallax is live on Google Play and the App Store.

Do I need a Discogs account?

Yes. Kallax syncs with your existing Discogs collection.
If you don't have one yet, create a free account at Discogs and start cataloging your records.

Is Kallax free?

Yes! Free includes full Discogs sync, BPM detection, 1 playlist, and 25 favorites.
Kallax Pro ($19.99) unlocks unlimited playlists, unlimited favorites, and data export.
One-time purchase. No subscriptions. Support an indie dev.

Works offline?

Completely. After initial sync, everything runs locally.
Dead venue WiFi, basement gigs, rooftop sets, you name it. Your collection always stays accessible without internet.

Is this replacing DJ software?

No. Kallax is not a DJ software. It's a companion for vinyl collectors and DJs. No beatmatching, no CDJ integration.
It handles collection management, BPM, playlists, and booth reference. Your turntables stay front and center.

How accurate is the BPM?

We use GetSongBPM which has millions of accurate songs. Coverage entirely depends on your collection. More obscure collectors will have fewer matches, but it's guaranteed to match a lot. When it misses, tap-tempo gets you accurate BPM in a few seconds.

What's included on the first release?

Everything. Discogs sync, BPM detection, playlist builder, and Live Mode are all working. This isn't a demo, it's pretty much the full product being given to you, and refined with your feedback.

What We Collect

  • Discogs Account: When you connect your Discogs account, we access your collection data to sync it with the app.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics to understand how the app is used and improve the experience.

How We Use Your Data

  • Sync your vinyl collection from Discogs
  • Improve the app based on user feedback
  • Provide customer support

What We Don't Do
